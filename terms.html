02The service

Limitless Trade Copier is desktop software for Windows that connects to one or more MetaTrader 5 ("MT5") trading accounts and replicates trade activity (open, modify, close orders) from a designated "master" account to one or more "slave" accounts according to rules you configure. The Service also includes related tools such as a prop-firm trading journal and per-account risk protection.

The Service does not place trades on your behalf based on its own judgement. It only mirrors decisions made by the human or algorithm controlling the master account. The Service is a tool, not a trading strategy.
